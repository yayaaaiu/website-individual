“Nothing is better than going home to family and eating good food and relaxing.” - Irina Shayk
Let's get to know my lovely family.

Hello!

My big family consists of 7 people which is my parents, 4 younger siblings and me. I am the oldest child and I have 2 younger brothers and 2 younger sisters. All of them are still in school and live with my parents. My mother works as an Immigration officer and my father is a taxi driver. My brothers and sisters still attending school in Putrajaya specifically Sekolah Rendah Kebangsaan Putrajaya Presint 14 (1) and Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Putrajaya Presint 14 (1).

They are staying in Putrajaya since my father works there, however we are in the process of moving to Kajang, Selangor since my mother got promoted to work at the Immigration department of the Kajang branch recently. My brothers and sisters are still going to stay at school in Putrajaya since it only takes about 30 minutes to arrive from Kajang and my brother will take SPM examination this year in march 2023.
